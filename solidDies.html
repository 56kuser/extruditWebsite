We design solid dies in many configurations, from dies and backers, to feeder die and backer sets. Our dies are designed for use in direct and indirect extrusion processes and for any type of aluminium alloy or application.

Our area of expertise include:

    • Heat sinks
    • Extremely wide extrusions using spreaded die technology
    • Zero bearing dies for small extrusion profiles
    • Single, double and triple pocket designs
    • Hard alloy dies with choked reverse bearings
    • Dies and backers with nitrogen cooling slots
    • Special single bearing length designs

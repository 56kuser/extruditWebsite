Semi Hollow Dies

Performance of Semi hollow dies are often impacted by poor tongue ratios.

We design semi hollow dies where tongue ratio is critical. To prevent tongue movement, we design dies and backers with a spigot in backers. Where spigots are not possible, we design them as Bridge in face or shut off dies. For hollow dies with sub optimal tongue ratio, we design the die with a covering mandrel.

Examples of semi hollow extrusions

    • Curtain channels
    • Hand rails

AboutUs

ExtrudiT Limited was formed to provide a comprehensive service to the gloabal aluminium extrusion industry. The core business of ExtrudiT Limited is Aluminium extrusion product development, die design, die correction, dies and tooling, training, courses, aluminium extrusion production optimization and consultancy services. We are experienced and dedicated to making your ideas take shape.
